About Me

I used to work as an ICU(Intensive Care Unit nurse) nurse for more than 3 years at SMC(Samsung Medical Center), one of the best hospitals in South Korea.

The time I spent in the hospital was truly precious; however, it made me want to experience a larger world.

I saw countless patients dying. Some of them were satisfied with their lives, but most of them weren't.

Watching them closely, such realization came to me that life is too short to hesitate and if I don't do what I want, I'll only regret it in the end.

So, I decided to quit my job and went to Canada to study English for a while.

Communication with diverse people helped widen my perspectives, which has led me to know that life is full of opportunities when I step outside my comfort zone.

Recently, I have become interested in Software and Web development which enables us to create something closely related to our life.

Thankfully, studying development has been a delight to me and I'm looking forward to the new challenge ahead as a web developer now.
